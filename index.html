Who are We?

We are a humble band of homeschoolers who simply love to read. Brought together by a shared curiosity and a love for stories, we meet regularly to connect, chat, and journey through books that spark imagination and thoughtful conversation.

Sometimes we explore timeless classics like Pride and Prejudice, and other times we dive into famous modern reads like Brave or Land of Stories. Whatever the book, each one brings fresh ideas to consider, in which we can discuss as a group, while having fun with it!

Our book club is created by and for homeschoolers in Fairview, and it’s open to all who enjoy reading and sharing that joy with others. Whether you're a seasoned bookworm or just starting to fall in love with reading, you’re welcome here.

About Us

What Makes Us Unique?

We are NOT your traditional book club? Sure, we are a teen group and we read books (obviously as this is a Book Club), but we also watch the movie adaptations if the book has them. So this group is could also be a movie club! We don't care about the page numbers (although some of us might, because of some of the lengths), we care about the story and how it made us feel.

We also meet up monthy, and if you look at the team structure below, that's where our uniqueness truly shines.

“Books are a uniquely portable magic.”
— Stephen King

What Makes Us Unique?

Ok so I promise I won't bore you, but you really have to see this.

Noa, since she's the founder, is the CEO. There are a group of staff members, known as the Council who has specific jobs, find out more in the Members Page. Noa's mom, Tita Sarah is, in a sense, our Grandmaster (or basically our adviser, I thought Grandmaster sounded cooler). She oversees the group. Every month, we meet up and choos a book to read for that month. We will all give out a book name and cast a vote. Whoever book wins, that guy/girl would be the leader for the month. Then, the next month, the whole process starts again.
