Welcome to Fairview Homeschooler's Book Club!

Who are We?

We are a humble band of homeschoolers who likes to read. We meet up, get to know each other, and dive into stories that spark curiosity, conversation, and connection. From classics like Little Women to new favorites like Barve or Resistance, every book we read brings something new to discuss, imagine, and learn together. Our club is situated and aimed for ALL Fairview Homeschoolers who likes to read. To find out more, go here. If you want to join, please head out here.
